# LinkedIn Post: AI Employees as FTEs - Present and Future

## Post Content

🤖 **The Future of Work is Here: AI Employees as Full-Time Equivalents**

The conversation around AI in the workplace has shifted. We're no longer asking "Can AI replace humans?" but rather "How can AI become a trusted team member?"

**The Reality Today:**
- AI agents can now handle 24/7 operations (vs. 40 hours/week for humans)
- Cost per task: ~$0.25-$0.50 (vs. $3-$6 for human FTEs)
- Ramp-up time: Instant (vs. 3-6 months for hiring)
- Consistency: 99%+ predictable (vs. 85-95% for humans)

**What This Means:**
✅ Routine tasks automated completely
✅ Human teams freed for strategic work
✅ 24/7 business continuity
✅ Exponential scaling without linear cost increases

**The Hybrid Future:**
The most powerful organizations won't choose between humans OR AI. They'll build teams of both—humans for judgment, creativity, and relationships; AI for execution, consistency, and scale.

Your next hire might not be a person. And that's not a threat—it's an opportunity.

What's your take? Are you already working with AI as a team member?
